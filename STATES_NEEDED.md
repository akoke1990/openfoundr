## How to contribute a state

1. Copy `data/states/_template.json`
2. Fill in your state's data (official sources only)
3. Submit a PR with title: `Add state: [State Name]`

No coding required. You can do the entire thing in your browser on GitHub. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for the full guide.
